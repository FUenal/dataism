Automated Fossil Fuel Policy Tracker

As part of my Data Science Fellowship with Faculty AI, I worked together with the The Fossil Fuel Non-Proliferation Treaty Initiative (FFNPT). The FFNPT Initiative is a global NGO empowering policy advisers, activists, and researchers to facilitate a rapid global transition away from fossil fuel toward clean energy.

The FFNPT Initiative has recently been endorsed by 101 Nobel Laureates and is thus rapidly gaining international traction.

I had the pleasure to work with the FFNPT on a Data Science project in which I programmed an interactive outline app, using web-scraping and Natural Language Processing (Artificial Neural Network) to automatically identify, filter, and visualize relevant policies globally.
